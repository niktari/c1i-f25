  • Harmonic Collection: Entry #2

    Last week you created your first programmed entry, using only HTML. This week, add an entry that uses HTML and CSS. Next week, we’ll look further at the design process and CSS, but for now, just experiment, have fun, and see where you can take your work this week. Think about how you’re growing the concept and expression of your collection. It’s okay to keep it simple. Think more about how you can have impactful and meaningful design choices, working within the limits of what you can execute today.

    Consider what HTML default styles you'd like to override or keep. Be intentional about it. Consider typography, and importing a custom font based on the take-home lecture you reviewed.

    Before you get started, create an entry-2 subfolder, and inside of it, create an index.html file and styles.css file. Note that you will need to do this for every entry moving forward.

    In your HTML file, make sure that you link to your CSS file by adding the below tag in your <head> section. This tag usually goes underneath your <title> tags:

    <link href ="styles.css" rel="stylesheet">

    Make sure this entry is linked out from your Harmonic Collection homepage. Don’t forget to commit your changes and push them to your live site. Double check that the changes have been reflected by visiting your username.github.io URL.
