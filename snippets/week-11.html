  • Random Styling Worksheet

    Complete the Random Styling Worksheet using CodePen. Create your own copy of the worksheet to work in by saving the original CodePen. Be sure to save your work along the way. You are permitted to work with other classmates on this worksheet, but you must submit your own CodePen link.

    1. I submitted a CodePen URL to my Random Styling Worksheet by 11/12 at 7 PM ET.

    2. I did my best to answer all of the questions. If I didn’t know the answer, I noted so in my code.

  • Harmonic Collection: Entry #8

    Create an index.html file, style.css file, and script.js file in your entry-8 folder. Make sure you link your script.js file in your index.html file by using the <script></script> tag! Reference last week's lecture slides for a refresher on how to do this.

    Design and develop an entry that either: 1) generates random content or 2) generates random styles. Or develop one that does both! How can the randomness be related to your collection’s theme? For instance, if your collection is a catalog of your favorite poems, can this entry be a random poem generator that pulls select lines from different poems?

    To get full credit, make sure you hit all the requirements in the checklist below.

    1. I submitted a link to my entry by 11/12 at 7 PM ET.

    2. I worked in the index.html, style.css, and script.js file of my entry-8 folder.

    3. I used JavaScript randomness to generate my content and/or styling.
