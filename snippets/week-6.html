  • Harmonic Collection: Entry #5

    Create your fifth entry for your Harmonic Collection. For this week, you should:

    1. Start in Figma. Work in the same design file that you have in this project space and work through your ideas. Make sure your name is in your file. Consider typography and layout: how can you break the top-to-bottom, left-to-right default layout of the page?
    2. Go to code. Create an index.html file and style.css file in your entry-5 folder. Translate your design in Figma to a live website using HTML and CSS. For this week, see if you can make your website responsive. Can you work with relative units? Can you make your entry look good on mobile? Additionally, think about responsiveness as a creative opportunity: do you want someone to have a similar or completely different experience based on their device? Depending on which route you take, how does it relate to your overall theme?

    Submit a link to your entry on Canvas for completion.

    To get full credit, make sure you hit all the requirements in the checklist below.

    1. I submitted a link to my entry by 10/14 at 7 PM ET.

    2. I worked in the index.html file and style.css file of my entry-5 folder.

    3. My entry is responsive and considers viewing experiences on different devices.
