  • Harmonic Collection: Final Refinement

    Congratulations, you’re almost there! Now that you have all of your entries in, take a look at your entire collection and do one last pass at it. Use this opportunity to improve at least 2-3 aspects of your website. For instance, is there a specific entry that you’d like to flesh out? Is there an overarching technical issue in your larger collection? Are you able to optimize the responsiveness of your overall website? Is there anything concept or design-wise that you can push?

    To get full credit, make sure you hit all the requirements in the checklist below.

    1. I submitted a link to my Harmonic Collection by 12/2 at 7 PM ET.

    2. I refined my concept, design, and/or code where applicable.

    3. Majority of my website is responsive.

  • Harmonic Collection: Final Presentation

    Based on feedback, refine your deck so it’s polished and presentation-ready by next class. Make sure that your deck is designed—don’t just keep the template styles!

    Practice your presentation so you can succinctly yet clearly share your work. Your presentation should be around 3-5 minutes in length.

    Make sure that your presentation includes:

    1. An overview of your collection’s theme.
    2. An overview of how your design decisions relate to the theme of your collection.
    3. A demonstration of your live site. Specifically, show us:
      1. How someone navigates the website.
      2. A deep dive of two entries that you think best represents the overall spirit of the collection. What is each entry about? How does each entry’s design relate to its content?
    4. Reflections on your work, which could include but are not limited to: ways you could extend the project beyond this class, technical pitfalls or achievements, or your favorite part about building your collection.

    To get full credit, make sure you hit all the requirements in the checklist below.

    1. I completed my final presentation by 12/2 at 7 PM ET.

    2. My deck outlines all of the points above.

    3. My deck is designed and presentation-ready.

    4. My file is in the [Harmonic Collection] 3 Ideas folder within the shared class team on Figma.
